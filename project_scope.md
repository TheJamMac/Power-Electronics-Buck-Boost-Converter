# Project Scope

In this project, I want to design and study DC-DC Buck and Boost converters.

My main goals are:

- to understand how Buck and Boost converters work
- to simulate both circuits in LTspice
- to observe voltage and current waveforms
- to compare converter behavior
- to look at efficiency and output voltage ripple
- to prepare the project for simple PCB design

At the beginning, I will start with simple converter circuits. After that, I will improve the design step by step.
